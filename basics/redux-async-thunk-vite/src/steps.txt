Preparing asynchronus thunk

1> Install react-redux, @reduxjs/toolkit
2> prepare a folder named "redux"
3> Prepare sub folders for actions and reducers
4> prepare the action with "createAsyncThunk"
5> prepare the reducer with "createSlice"
6> prepare the store with "configStore"

How to execute
from component

1> import useSelector hook and useDispath hook from react-redux
